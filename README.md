# Cleyo — AI-Powered Task Management

Cleyo is a task manager that handles the thinking for you. No more manual triaging, tagging, or rescheduling — brain-dump your tasks in plain English and let Cleyo's AI organize your day.

## Commands

### Add Task
Type naturally and Cleyo extracts deadlines, time estimates, priorities, and categories:

- *"Draft the investor deck by Friday at 2pm, takes about 3 hours, high priority"*
- *"Buy groceries tonight"*
- *"Research competitor pricing next week"*

### Today
An AI-prioritized daily plan based on your current energy level.

- **High Energy** — tackle the big rocks and complex projects.
- **Medium Energy** — balance progress with quick wins.
- **Low Energy** — clear out administrative debt and easy tasks.

### Search Tasks
Keyboard-first interface to search, filter, and organize your entire task history.

### Send Feedback
Report bugs, request features, or share general feedback directly with the Cleyo team without leaving Raycast.

### Sign Out
Securely disconnect the linked Cleyo account.

## Setup

1. Create an account at [cleyo.app](https://cleyo.app).
2. Open any Cleyo command in Raycast.
3. Follow the link to connect your account.
4. Start capturing.

## Privacy

Cleyo processes task metadata with AI to organize and plan your day. Your task content is encrypted in transit and never sold to third parties.
